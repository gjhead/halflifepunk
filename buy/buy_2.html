Package includes full-color insert, pictures, extensive liner notes and song lyrics.

This disc compiles material from the 1986 Under the Knife 7", the extremely rare What’s Left cassette, and contains several unreleased live tracks.

The lineup features the original 1985-87 members: Jeff Lamm, Mike LaVella (of Gearhead records), Vince Curtis and a pre-Don Caballero Damon Che.

Half Life combined the sonic fury of British punk bands such as Discharge and Broken Bones with Faith/Void style lyrics.

Half Life played heavily in the eastern US and released several records in the mid 80's.

Atypical of the genre, always original and insightful, Half Life were often called "one of the heaviest bands" of their day.
